# Product School - Course Portfolio Assessment

Public, shareable mirror of the Product School certification portfolio assessment.

The page is a synthesis of seven Product School certifications - the three AI certs (Vibe Coding, AI Product Management, AI Product Strategy) plus the four core product certs tracked via feedback (Product Management Certificate, Product Leader Certificate, Data Product Manager Certificate, Product Marketing Certificate) - pairing weekly student and instructor feedback with the canonical course packages to produce sized, sequenced editorial decisions.

## Live page

https://djn-krs-2709.github.io/ps-course-portfolio-assessment/

## Source

The canonical assessment lives in the private `Product-School-Platform/ps-content-library` repository at `outputs/assessments/course-portfolio.html`. This repository is a publish-only mirror of that file.

The cert registry that drives ingest, digest, and assessment is at `config/certifications.json` in the canonical repo. Adding a new course to the portfolio is a single edit there.

## Refresh cadence

After every Monday digest, and before any roadmap conversation about course revisions.
